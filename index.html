Sushi Maru Spokane, WA

About Us

Sushi Maru invites you to enjoy our kaiten-zushi dining experience* serving both traditional and creative sushi in a relaxed and friendly atmosphere. “Maru” means round or perfection. Our extensive menu features a wide variety of the high quality sushi and sushi rolls, as well as tataki, udon, cooked appetizers and desserts for everyone. The selection is not limited to sushi…see our menu. We are proud of our professional and exceptionalservice that we extend to each of our guests. Great with kids and beginner sushi eaters; we not only offer a comprehensive sushi menu but also a wide selection of non-sushi/seafood items such as Honey Walnut Shrimp or even Popcorn Chicken.

  • Gyoza — Fried dumplings stuffed with shredded pork

Light Blue Plates are $2.25

  • Ika — Raw squid over a bed of fresh steamed rice
  • Salmon Nigiri — Raw salmon over a bed of fresh steamed rice

Red Plates are $2.75

  • Maru Veggie Roll — Sliced cucumber, avocado, pickled radish, Yamagobo, and Kampyo Vegetarian Dish
  • Maguro — Raw Maguro (Yellowfin Tuna) over a bed of fresh steamed rice

Purple Plates are $3.25

  • Spider Roll — Tempura fried soft shell crab with avocado, cucumber, imitation crab, Kaiware (Daikon Sprouts), and Masago to garnish
  • Udon Noodle Soup — Soy broth, Udon noodles, and steamed fish cakes

Dark Blue Plates are $3.75

  • Godzilla Roll — Raw spicy tuna roll topped with Unagi, Tobiko, green onions and chili sauce
  • Rainbow Roll — California roll topped with salmon, tuna, Izumidai, and avocado

Green Plates are $4.50

  • Uni — Raw sea urchin over a bed of fresh steamed rice
  • Tuna Sashimi — Four slices of tuna served raw
